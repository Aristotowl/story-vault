# The Only Way Out is Through

## Summary

A young owlet wants to play with her friends at the park but there is a dark forest path in the way.
She has taken it many times with her father but is scared to do it on her own.
She chats with "Light" and "Dark" in her mind and eventually Light convinces her that the reward is worth the risk.
Light also explains that it is good to listen to your "Fear" emotions. 
Many generations of family use this emotion to try and keep you safe from dangers but Dark works to corrupt it to create despair.

### Section 1: Friends ask Luna to play

- Luna's friends come over and ask if she wants to go to the park.
- She runs in to ask her dad but when she comes back they are already at the park.
- She runs up to the forest path but the dark scares her.
- She runs back inside to ask dad if he will take her, but he is busy and reminds her that they have taken this path many times and it is safe.

### Section 2: Meet "Light" and "Dark"

- Luna goes back outside and walks up to the forest path.
- She is really scared and sad she cannot play with her friends.
- "Light" shows up and ask's her what is wrong. She explains and "Light" tries to convince her that it is ok.
- Just when she is almost ready to go, "Dark" shows up to scare her again.

### Section 3: Meet "Fear"

- Light explains that "Fear" is a gift given to us by our generations of families past.
- It is used by Light to help keep you safe, but can be corrupted by Dark to bring despair.
- It is her job to learn when Dark is corrupting Fear.
- This takes years of experience and thinking about the risks and rewards.
- Light has some tips on how to decide when fear is brought on by light or dark

### Section 4: The battle between "Light" and "Dark"

- Light will always be there to try and keep Fear from getting corrupted by Dark.
- Dark is powerful and if you don't work at it he will win.
- Light and Dark go back and forth trying to convince Fear what to think
    - Picture of "Fear" with Light on the left and Dark on the right and their arguments below them.

### Section 5: Light wins and fear is calmed and Joy comes to say hi

- Luna looks into her past about the many times she has crossed the forest path with her father and realizes that it isn't so scary.
- Fear starts to change, calming down.
- She makes the decision to cross the path, and when she gets through, Dark and Fear have left and Joy makes an appearance.
- Final scene shows her running to her friends with Light and Joy shining bright
