# Aristotowl and the Bitcoin Battle

## Section One - Character and story intros

- Scene 1:  Dark grungy bar where the antagonists are meeting to discuss a plan to destroy Bitcoin.
  - We are introduced to:
    - Barry Bensler: Stupid regulator
    - Ms. Warrent: Corrupt politician
    - Mr. Diamond: Powerful banker
    - Don Doomer: Local news anchor
  - Narrated by Ms. Warrent
- Scene 2: Classical education charter school named Owl Valor
  - We are introduced to:
    - Aristotowl: Wise old owl who teaches at the school
    - Alex: Oldest and leader of the owlets, brains and brawn
    - sister: Youngest and most empathic and feminine 
    - brother: Middle, artistic, brains and brawn
  - Narrated by Aristotowl as he chats with 3 young owlets about Bitcoin
