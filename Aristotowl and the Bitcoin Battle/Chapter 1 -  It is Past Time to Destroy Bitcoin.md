# Chapter 1: It is Past Time to Destroy Bitcoin

## Scene 1: Rat's Tail

    Ms. Warrent was sitting in her usual spot at the Rat's Tail, the dingiest bar in town. She was here to meet with Barry and Mr. Diamond, but they were both late. She hated waiting. Nothing made her more angry then having to sit in her own thoughts. 
    
    She started to remember when she was a junior senator and helped champion a new law that sounded wonderful and had so much potential but ended up a complete failure. The "No Owlet will Starve Again" act. It wasn't her fault though. She was new and didn't realize the name's of the bills almost always did the opposite of what they were titled. Plus, the idiot regulators botched the implementation of it. Not to mention all the parasites forcing kick backs for their districts in order to vote for it. Yes, she got some money for an environmental project in her district, but that was needed....right?

    "Ms. Warrent. Is something wrong?" asked the waiter
    "No!" she huffed. "Why would something be wrong?"
    "I apologize. You just had a look and I wanted to make sure everything was too your satisfaction."
    "Well....you could get me another drink. I have been empty for a while now."
    "Of course, right away." The waiter thankfully took his leave.

    "What a jerk!" Ms. warren thought. How could he possibly know what was going through her head. And didn't he know who she was! A senior senator. One of the most powerful owls in the land! "I might have to write a new law to tax these types of places if they don't start showing more respect!"

    Just then the door opened and it was Barry. "Oh great....now I have to entertain this idiot" she thought. "If he would have just listened to us when we told him to ban it, we wouldn't have to be meeting here today!"
    "Hi Liz!" Barry yelled from across the room.
    "Shut up and get over here!" She hated when he called her that! And, why was he in such a good mood. There is nothing good about having to do this now, with all the momentum in the wrong direction. Plus this was a secret meeting!
    "Keep your voice down!" she warned "You know we aren't supposed to be meeting about this!"
    "Oh yeah! I forgot about that part." Barry pulled up a chair and sat down next to Ms. Warrent. "So.....how is your day going?" he asked cautiously.
    The look she gave him made Barry turn away. "Good" she thought "Now maybe he will be quite."
    Thankfully at that moment the door opened and their benefactor entered the bar.

    Mr. Diamond is a short average looking owl with a little extra weight around the midsection. But, don't let his looks fool you. He is as dangerous as a shark with the brains to match. As the owner of the largest bank in Sagetree Hollow, he had more power than almost anyone.
    He was one of a very few owls Ms. Warrent was afraid of.

    Mr. Diamond strutted over to their table and took a seat. "Ah Barry, it is so wonderful to see you again. I trust you've recovered from the... challenges of our last project? I must say, it was quite the ordeal, especially since you managed to find a way to complicate matters."
    
    "Nice to see you too Mr. Diamond!" replied Barry.

    "Ms. Warrent. How are you on this lovely afternoon?" Mr. Diamond tipped his hat in her direction.

    What did that mean? Was he actually trying to be nice or was it an insult like the greeting to Barry. She never understood this part of the game.
    "I am fine. Let's get on with it."

    "All business as usual. Alright then here it is: It is past time to destroy Bitcoin and this is how we are going to do it."
